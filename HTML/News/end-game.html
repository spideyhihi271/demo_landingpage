Review movie Avengers End Game – The end of the beginning

By Mavel

Review of the movie Avengers End Game – is the movie that ended the entire Avengers superhero series that was released in 2012. And since then the MCU has affirmed its position in the market and brought the Marvel universe to the next level. new . Dominate almost all markets in that moment.

In the article below, I will learn with you a brief overview of the content and review of the avengers end game movie. Let's follow the article below!

More than a year after Thanos' snap wiped half of all life in the universe, including beloved superheroes like Black Panther, Spider Man, Dr. Strange, our Avengers have made a sensational comeback .

Quiet, emotional , but no less witty

From Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, etc., all had to experience bitter defeat and torment in pain because of the loss of their loved ones. In their hearts, they always struggle to find all the ways, even the smallest with the faint hope of resurrecting the dead: "Whatever it takes" (At any cost) .

Coming to Endgame, every Marvel fan is looking forward, nervously wondering how , the superheroes can turn the tide and defeat Thanos, because the crazy Titan is too strong. Hope was only glimmered by the "big sister" Captain Marvel with the same power as the cosmic entity that was "summoned" in time by Nick Fury before he "returned to dust".

The epic party is enough to make you "goosebumps" – review the movie avengers end game

Commenting on an MCU movie without mentioning the image, sound, and effects is really a big mistake. Endgame is exactly a grand party, enough to make you "goosebumps", lightheaded with excitement. If the battle at Wakanda in Infinity War ever made you "admiring", prepare your hands to clap and your mouth to cheer because Endgame certainly has ears and eyes many times over.

Combined with the melodious, deep melodies that are resounded in emotional segments , sometimes majestic and vibrant in intense action scenes , and a series of familiar songs that have been created. Famous in the previous Marvel movies , Endgame really understands how to pamper fans.

Of course, it is a continuation of the events of the previous movies , so to be able to feel the full beauty of Endgame, you really need to watch at least 2 movies, Civil War and Infinity War. If you haven't seen any Avengers movies, you will have a hard time capturing the emotional moments, the witty jokes... the quintessence of the work.

Sound, images, effects – review movie avengers end game

Avengers: Endgame is really a party when it comes to giving the audience impressive footage . All the designs are meticulously cared for, making the audience overwhelmed with the scenes of superheroes appearing . The battle phase with a combination of techniques makes viewers only know to enjoy without blinking.
